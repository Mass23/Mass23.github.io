My artworks


Projects are structured by thematic focus and medium. Key areas of investigation include:

1. Identity and Social Constructs

Analysis of identity formation processes and their dependence on societal structures. The objective is to expose and critique the mechanisms that shape individual and collective identity.

2. Activism?

Projects in this category address themes of fairness, environmental responsibility, and the promotion of open and inclusive systems. Outcomes are intended to highlight disparities and propose alternative modes of engagement.

3. Integration of Scientific Methodologies

Exploration of the overlap between scientific and artistic methodologies. By incorporating scientific tools and frameworks into artistic processes, these works aim to dissolve the perceived separation between these domains.
