About


Hello world (actually ~67% of it).

This is where I display most of the things I like doing. Feel free to browse, share, etc. My work revolves around the creation of digital material through various mediums but always involving either programming or music composition, or both. I also like to use screens, sound systems, projectors, and prints to put them into space to engage the audience.

I try and create new aesthetics that I find interesting, and play with conceptual ideas. I also like to challenge political views and social constructs. I also participate in art projects with my collective Brodinsky sur Boue (https://brodinskysurboue.ch/)

If you want to reach out feel free to contact me via e-mail (massimo.bourquin@gmail.com), instagram(@massimobourquin), or pass by Lausanne (Switzerland).
