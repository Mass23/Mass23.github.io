ABOUTE


Hello world.

This is where I display most of the things I like doing. I use it mostly as a repository for myself, but feel free to browse, share, repurpose, etc. My work revolves around the creation of digital material through various mediums but always involving either programming (to create visuals, and interactive displays) or music composition, or both. I also like to use screens, sound systems, projectors, and prints to put them into space to engage the audience.

During the day, I work as a researcher in the field of alpine microbial ecology, studying the microbes that inhabit high-altitude ecosystems.

I try and create new aesthetics that I find interesting, and play with conceptual ideas. I also like to challenge political views and social constructs. I also participate in cool art projects with my collective Brodinsky sur Boue (https://brodinskysurboue.ch/)

If you want to reach out feel free to contact me via e-mail (massimo.bourquin@gmail.com), instagram(@massimobourquin), or pass by Lausanne (Switzerland).
