My artworks


Programmatic art leverages computational processes to generate artistic outcomes. Using code and programs, I create works that integrate visuals, sound, and interactivity, operating at the intersection of technology and aesthetics. These works challenge the historical division between art and science by repurposing a technical medium for artistic expression.

My practice also includes glitch art, internet art, sound design, and audiovisual compositions. Projects often combine multiple mediums to achieve outcomes tailored to specific conceptual frameworks. Code is used as a functional tool to manipulate digital artifacts, model abstract systems, and produce results that align with the intended goals of the work.
